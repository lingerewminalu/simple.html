Its likely that anyone who uses e-Learning will tell you how impactful and convenient it is. In todays “always-on” world, eLearning offers many benefits to both learners and organizations. But what is eLearning, exactly? Whether youre an eLearning expert or beginner, theres no denying its popularity has skyrocketed over the past decade. For employers, it offers an affordable, time-efficient way to train employees. In return, employees benefit from convenient training that enhances their skills and knowledge. Its also used by organizations to train their partners and customers to increase adoption, retention, and ultimately, revenue.
